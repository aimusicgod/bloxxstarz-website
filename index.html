Who We Are

About BloxxStarz

We are raising creators. Not just consumers.

BloxxStarz is a youth entertainment, education, and media platform built for the next generation — the kids who are already gaming, creating, performing, and dreaming big. We give them the tools, training, and stage to turn those passions into real skills, real music, and real opportunities.

Founded by Monyee Morton and operated under Eleven One Media Group LLC, BloxxStarz brings together music, digital content, gaming, performing arts, visual arts, filmmaking, AI, and life skills — all in one place, all designed for youth ages 7–16.

We are not a school. We are not just a music group. BloxxStarz is a movement — a fully integrated youth creator ecosystem where young people don't just consume entertainment, they make it.

⭐

Our debut single drops June 19, 2026 — Juneteenth — marking the official launch of a platform built to change how the world develops young talent.

"BloxxStarz is a youth creative ecosystem that integrates performing arts, visual arts, music, gaming, filmmaking, AI, and life guidance to develop the next generation of creators in a safe and structured environment."

Our Mission

The Founder

Monyee Morton

Founder & Executive Director · BloxxStarz

An entertainment manager, life coach, youth developer, and Ayurvedic practitioner, Monyee built BloxxStarz from a simple but powerful belief: every child deserves a stage, a skill, and a safe space to grow. She recognized what most entertainment companies were missing — a platform that didn't just showcase young talent, but actually developed it. She is also the founder of Garden of iAM and author of Raising Digital Athletes — a parent's guide to safe gaming, computer literacy, and structured digital play — further proof of her commitment to empowering not just youth, but the families who raise them.

"We are raising creators, not just consumers."
